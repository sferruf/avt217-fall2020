E1: 20 Questions for Sofia Ferrufino

Question 1

Q: What is your favorite type of food?

A: Italian food

Question 2

Q: What is your zodiac sign?

A: Scorpio

Question 3

Q: If you could live anywhere, where would it be?

A: France or Spain.

Question 4

Question 5

Question 6
